### น้ำหยด

แอดมินแนะนำให้ช่างเข้าไปตรวจเช็คก่อนนะคะ ค่าตรวจเช็ค 700 บาท ถ้ามีซ่อมต่อสามารถนำไปรวมกับค่าซ่อมได้ค่ะ

### แพง

แอดมิน:

ถ้าแอร์ไม่มีอาการหนัก เลือกล้างปกติ 550 ได้ค่ะ

## English

### Price
Customer: How much is aircon cleaning?  
Admin:
Our wall-type aircon cleaning starts from 550 THB for standard cleaning.  
The final price depends on the BTU size and cleaning type.  
May I know how many units you have?

### Water leak
Customer: My aircon is leaking water.  
Admin:
For water leaking, we usually recommend hang-coil deep cleaning because it allows the technician to clean the rear drain tray and drain path more thoroughly.  
It helps reduce the chance of the leak coming back. May I check your location and preferred date?

### Not cold
Customer: My aircon is not cold.  
Admin:
It may be caused by dirt buildup, low refrigerant, leakage, outdoor unit problem, sensor, board, or compressor issue.  
We recommend a diagnostic inspection first. The inspection fee starts from 700 THB, and if you proceed with the repair, it can be included in the repair cost.
